Abstract Overview

SujalPOS represents a paradigm shift in restaurant hospitality technology, transitioning from a reactive transactional tool to a proactive intelligence engine. By integrating Large Language Models (LLM), autonomous Multi-Agent Systems (MAS), and statistical forecasting, it empowers restaurateurs with real-time semantic querying and data-driven operational strategies. The architecture seamlessly bridges high-speed POS operations with deep analytical insight.

Advanced Intelligence Features

Ask AI (NLP Chatbot)

A natural language interface allowing staff to query sales, inventory, and wastage using plain English. Powered by a hybrid intent-classification pipeline and Groq-hosted Llama 3 models.

Revenue Intelligence Engine

A sophisticated module performing high-fidelity margin analysis, menu engineering (BCG Matrix), and association analysis to recommend automated combos and price optimizations.

Multi-Agent Planning (CrewAI)

Four autonomous agents (Sales, Inventory, Pricing, and Co-Pilot) collaborate daily to synthesize complex data into a unified, actionable morning brief for the restaurant owner.

Predictive Sales Forecasting

Utilizes Degree-2 Polynomial Regression via NumPy to simulate and forecast future revenue trends based on historical cyclicality and growth patterns.

Core Operational Modules

Omni-Channel KDS

A unified Kitchen Display System handling dine-in, takeaway, and simulated Zomato/Swiggy orders with real-time preparation timers and status synchronization.

Dynamic Inventory Mgmt

Real-time stock tracking with Bill of Materials (BOM) linking. Includes auto-deduction, reorder level alerts, and AI-predicted stockout horizons.

Automated Billing & GST

Comprehensive fiscal module generating compliant PDF invoices, handling multi-mode payments (UPI/Cash/Card), and automated email dispatch.

Eva: Voice Call Order AI

A Twilio-integrated voice agent capable of answering phone calls and taking orders directly into the POS system using Whisper and Groq APIs.
